Frequently Asked Questions

Does the app require an internet connection?

No. All phrases, audio, and translations are stored on your device. The app works 100% offline. Internet is only needed for purchasing the premium version.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Go to Settings > Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

How do I change my language?

Go to Settings and select your native language from the language picker. The app supports Russian, Ukrainian, Spanish, Hindi, Polish, Chinese, French, Arabic, and Portuguese.

How do I delete my data?

Go to Settings > Delete All Data. This removes your profile, favorites, and preferences. Alternatively, uninstalling the app deletes everything.

The audio is not playing. What should I do?

Make sure your device is not in Silent Mode (check the physical switch on the side of your iPhone). Also check that the volume is turned up.

Can I suggest new phrases?

Absolutely! Send us an email with your phrase suggestions and we will consider adding them in a future update.
