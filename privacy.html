Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Overview

English for Delivery ("the App") is designed with your privacy as a priority. We collect no personal data, use no analytics services, and make no network requests beyond Apple's own StoreKit for in-app purchases.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics sent to external servers
  • Crash reports sent to third parties

Data Stored Locally

The App stores the following data only on your device:

  • Your display name (optional, entered during onboarding)
  • Language and transport preferences
  • Favorite phrases
  • App settings (theme, audio speed)
  • Local usage counters (stored in UserDefaults, never transmitted)

This data is stored using Apple's SwiftData framework and UserDefaults. It never leaves your device.

In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time lifetime premium purchase processed entirely through Apple's StoreKit. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs all purchase transactions: apple.com/legal/privacy

Third-Party Services

The App uses no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or tracking frameworks. All functionality is built using Apple's native frameworks.

Data Deletion

You can delete all locally stored data at any time from Settings > Delete All Data within the App. Alternatively, uninstalling the App removes all local data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since the App collects no data at all, it is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
